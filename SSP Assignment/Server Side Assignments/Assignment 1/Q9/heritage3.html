Gandhi Hall

Mahatma Gandhi Town Hall is a historic building in Indore, a city in the central Indian state of Madhya Pradesh . This building dates back to the British era and at that time it also used to tell the time and was also called Ghantaghar. Various types of cultural programs are organized in this building throughout the year. [1] The building was constructed in 1904 and was then named King Edward Hall. After India became independent in 1947, it was named Gandhi Hall. The hall also has a park for children and a library.
