RalaMandal Wildlife Sanctury

The Ralamandal Wildlife Sanctuary was established in 1989 in Indore, Madhya Pradesh. It is spread over five square kilometers and is home to different species of birds and other wildlife.[1][2] It has an ancient palace built by Holkar which was primarily used as a hunting hut also known as shikargah (hunting lodge).
